    Our Lawn Care Services

    At Precision Lawns, we're here for you whenever you need lawn care in Fulton County, IL.

    Our regular lawn maintenance service keeps your yard looking its best throughout the year. We provide consistent mowing, edging, and trimming to ensure a neat and tidy appearance, along with seasonal care such as leaf removal and debris cleanup. Trust us to maintain the health and beauty of your lawn with professional care and attention to detail.

    Our professional seeding service helps fill in bare spots and thickens your lawn for a lush, green appearance. We use premium grass seed tailored to your lawn's specific needs, ensuring robust growth and improved lawn health.

    Aeration improves the health of your lawn by allowing air, water, and nutrients to penetrate the soil more effectively. Our core aeration process reduces soil compaction, enhances root development, and promotes a stronger, more resilient lawn.

    Our lawn fertilization service ensures your grass gets the essential nutrients it needs to thrive. Using high-quality, eco-friendly fertilizers, we promote healthy growth, vibrant color, and increased resistance to pests and diseases.
